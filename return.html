🔄 Returns Policy

We want you to be 100% satisfied with your purchase. Here's how our returns work.

7-Day Easy Returns

We offer a 7-day return policy from the date of delivery. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 7 days.

✅ 100% Money Back Guarantee - If you're not happy, we'll refund your money. No questions asked!

Conditions for Returns

  • Product must be unused - Items must be in original condition
  • Original packaging - Return in the original box/package
  • Within 7 days - Return within 7 days of delivery
  • Proof of purchase - Order ID or invoice required

Note: Products damaged due to misuse are not eligible for return.

Items That Cannot Be Returned

  • 🚫 Customized or personalized items
  • 🚫 Perishable goods (food, plants, etc.)
  • 🚫 Hygiene products (underwear, socks, etc.)
  • 🚫 Digital products (software, e-books, etc.)
  • 🚫 Items damaged by the customer
